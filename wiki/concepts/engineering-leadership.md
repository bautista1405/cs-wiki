# Engineering Leadership

The art and science of leading technical teams and shaping the technical direction of an organization.

## Key Dimensions
- **Scope of Impact**: Moving from individual tasks to team goals and organizational outcomes.
- **Influence**: Leading through expertise, mentorship, and strategic alignment rather than just formal authority.
- **Staff Engineering**: High-level individual contribution that requires deep technical skill combined with systemic organizational thinking.
