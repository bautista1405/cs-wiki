# Source: UIUC CS 225 - Data Structures and Algorithms
Author: University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
Year: 2023
URL: https://courses.grainger.illinois.edu/cs225/sp2023/
Summary: Academic course providing a comprehensive introduction to data structures and algorithms using C++.

## Key Contributions
- Structured pedagogical approach to DSA.
- Comprehensive coverage of fundamental structures (Trees, Heaps, Hash Tables) and algorithms (Dijkstra, MST).
- Strong emphasis on memory management and C++ specifics (Inheritance, Templates, Stack/Heap).

## Bias and Gaps
- **Counter-arguments**: Academic curricula often prioritize theoretical correctness and standard implementations over industrial-scale performance optimizations.
- **Data gaps**: Course materials are specific to the 2023 spring semester; may not include the most recent advancements in the field.
