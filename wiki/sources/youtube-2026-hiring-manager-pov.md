# Hiring Manager POV

Perspectives from the other side of the interview table.

## Key Insights
- Hiring managers look for signal on autonomy, ownership, and reliability.
- Communication skills are often the deciding factor between two technically equal candidates.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: "Culture fit" can be a mask for unconscious bias.
- **Data gaps**: The POV is specific to the speaker's industry and company size.
