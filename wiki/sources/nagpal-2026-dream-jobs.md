# Ankur Nagpal - Applying for Dream Jobs

Tactics for reaching out and getting noticed by high-tier companies.

## Key Insights
- Direct outreach to employees/managers is more effective than applying through portals.
- Showcasing proof of work (portfolio) is a stronger signal than a resume.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Direct outreach requires an existing network or high social capital.
- **Data gaps**: Efficiency of this method for entry-level vs. senior engineers.
