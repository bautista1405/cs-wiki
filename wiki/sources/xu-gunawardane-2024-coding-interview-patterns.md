# Coding Interview Patterns (2024)

## Summary
A comprehensive guide to mastering coding interviews by focusing on 19 essential algorithmic patterns. Emphasizes pattern recognition over memorizing individual problems.

## Key Takeaways
- Pattern recognition is more valuable than solving a high volume of random problems.
- Focus on mapping a problem to a known pattern (e.g., Sliding Window for substrings).
- Importance of Big O complexity analysis for every solution.
- Managing edge cases such as integer overflow and geometric precision (using GCD).

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Some argue that over-reliance on patterns can lead to a lack of first-principles thinking when facing a truly novel problem.
- **Data gaps**: The source focuses on algorithmic patterns but does not cover system design or behavioral interview patterns in detail.
