# Ethan Evans - Promotions

Guidelines and perspectives on navigating the promotion process.

## Key Insights
- Promotions are not automatic; they require a demonstrable shift in impact and scope.
- Documenting wins and maintaining a "brag document" is essential.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Promotion systems can be political and opaque regardless of performance.
- **Data gaps**: Focuses more on the idea of promotion than the specific rubric differences between levels.
