# Coding Interview

The coding interview is a technical assessment aimed at evaluating a candidate's problem-solving ability, algorithmic knowledge, and communication skills.

## Key Preparation Frameworks
- **Pattern Recognition**: Shifting from memorizing problems to recognizing underlying patterns (e.g., Sliding Window, Two Pointers).
- **Thinking Aloud**: The process of communicating the thought process during a live coding session to allow interviewers to provide hints and assess reasoning.
- **Time & Space Complexity**: Rigorous application of Big O notation to evaluate efficiency.
