Focuses on the *framework* of the interview:
- **Step 1: Requirements**: Functional vs Non-functional requirements (availability, scalability, latency).
- **Step 2: API Design**: Defining the interface for the system.
- **Step 3: Database Schema**: Choosing the right storage and indexing.
- **Step 4: High Level Design**: Block diagrams of components.
- **Step 5: Deep Dive**: Focusing on specific bottlenecks (e.g., how to handle 1M concurrent users).
