# Mochary Method Curriculum

This document serves as a curriculum for the Mochary Method.

## Key Sections
- **Coach**: Includes discussions on whether CEO coaching is worth it, how to find a coach, and the difference between a coach, mentor, consultant, and cheerleader.
- **Social Good**: Focuses on themes like "Make Money, Have Fun, Do Good" and the Mochary Foundation.
- **Matt in Action**: Links to videocasts and podcasts (e.g., Jason Calacanis, Harry Stebbings).
- **FAQ**: Q&A with Matt Mochary.
- **Blog Posts**: Various articles on the stock market, remote work, and specific tool setups (e.g., Notion).
- **1-1 Meeting Format**: Provides templates and instructions for 1-1 meetings, including specific templates for coachees and team meetings.
