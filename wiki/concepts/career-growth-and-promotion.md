# Career Growth and Promotion

The systematic progression of an engineer's role, scope, and compensation within an organization.

## Growth Drivers
- **Impact and Scope**: Moving from individual tasks to systemic organizational outcomes.
- **Proof of Work**: Documenting achievements and maintaining visible impact.
- **Leveling Frameworks**: Understanding the explicit rubrics used for promotion.
- **Entrepreneurial Agility**: Rapid iteration, obsession with Product Market Fit (PMF), and the ability to pivot based on market signals.
- **Strategic Hype**: Using public awareness and "hype" to create visibility, especially for new products or roles.


## Promotion Tactics
- **Brag Documents**: Systematic recording of wins to facilitate review.
- **Strategic Alignment**: Ensuring work aligns with the goals of leadership.
- **Self-Assessment**: Regular auditing of growth gaps against expectations.
