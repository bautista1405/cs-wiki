    13|Mock interviews are the final stage of preparation. Solving 500 LeetCode problems alone is different from explaining the solution to a human in real-time.
    14|
    15|### Benefits
    16|- **Pressure Simulation**: Managing anxiety and 'brain freeze'.
    17|- **Feedback Loop**: Getting an external perspective on communication clarity and code quality.
    18|- **Pramp/Peer-to-Peer**: Platforms that allow candidates to interview each other.
    19|
    20|### Key Focus Areas
    21|- Timing management.
    22|- Articulating thought processes without stuttering.
    23|- Handling hint from interviewers.
