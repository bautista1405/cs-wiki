# Ashish Pratap Singh - Dream Jobs

Strategies for targeting and landing high-impact roles.

## Key Insights
- Researching the specific needs of a company before applying is key.
- Tailoring the approach to solve a problem the company actually has.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: This "surgical" approach is higher effort and may not scale for volume applicants.
- **Data gaps**: Success rates for this method compared to standard applications are not quantified.
