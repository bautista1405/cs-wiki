The la-ha moment of this source is the breadth of the 'primer'. It covers:
- **Core Components**: DNS, Load Balancers, Caching, Databases (SQL vs NoSQL).
- **Scaling**: Horizontal vs Vertical scaling.
- **Consistency**: CAP Theorem (Consistency, Availability, Partition Tolerance) and PACELC.
- **Network**: TCP/UDP, HTTP, gRPC.
- **System Thinking**: Designing for failure (redundancy, replication).
