# Dropbox Career Framework

Publicly shared rubric for engineering growth and expectations.

## Key Insights
- Clear definitions of what is expected at each level (Junior to Principal).
- Focus on "impact" as the primary driver for leveling.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Frameworks are prescriptive and may not cover all types of high-impact work.
- **Data gaps**: Does not explain the "how" of achieving these goals, only the "what".
