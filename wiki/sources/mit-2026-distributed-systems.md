# Summary: 6.5840 Distributed Systems

MIT's **6.5840 (formerly 6.824)** is a graduate-level course focusing on the engineering of distributed systems. It emphasizes the transition from theoretical abstractions to practical implementation.

The curriculum covers critical challenges in distributed environments:
- **Fault Tolerance**: Ensuring the system continues to operate despite individual node failures.
- **Replication**: Maintaining copies of data across multiple nodes.
- **Consistency**: Ensuring that all nodes see the same data state.

## Counter-arguments
