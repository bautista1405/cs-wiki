# Concurrent and Distributed Systems (Cambridge)

## Summary
Course materials from the University of Cambridge (2021-22) covering both concurrent and distributed systems.

## Key Content
- **Concurrent Systems**: Focus on primitives, scheduling, transactions, and lock-free structures.
- **Distributed Systems**: Focus on replication, consistency guarantees, and the Raft consensus algorithm.
