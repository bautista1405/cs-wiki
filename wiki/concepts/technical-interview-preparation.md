# Technical Interview Preparation

The process of preparing for and navigating technical evaluations in the software industry.

## Core Components
- **DSA Mastery**: Systematic study of algorithms and data structures.
- **Behavioral Signaling**: Communicating ownership, autonomy, and "culture fit".
- **Market Strategy**: Targeting companies based on levels, compensation, and role alignment.

## Strategic Approaches
- **Structured Study**: Utilizing comprehensive curricula.
- **Surgical Application**: Targeted outreach and problem-solving for specific companies.
- **POV Alignment**: Understanding the hiring manager's goals to reduce uncertainty.
