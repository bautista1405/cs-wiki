# UNIX Weapons School (Nick Black)

## Summary
An advanced elective practicum designed to train competent systems programmers through a rigorous curriculum of hardware and software internals.

## Curriculum Highlights
- **Computer Architecture**: CMOS power, pipelining, superscalars, and the x86 instruction set.
- **Memory and I/O**: Virtual memory, TLB invalidation, RDMA, and zero-copy I/O patterns.
- **Algorithmic Efficiency**: Constant sorts, PATRICIA tries, and event system algorithms.
- **Compilers**: SSA, basic blocks, and the limits of compiler-based optimization.
- **Parallelism**: Hardware vs. software parallelism, POSIX threads, and coroutines.
