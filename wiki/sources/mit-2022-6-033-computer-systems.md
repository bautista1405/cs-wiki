# 6.033: Computer Systems Engineering (MIT)

## Summary
MIT course on systems engineering, emphasizing modularity, abstraction, and the impact of systems.

## Key Modules
- **Operating Systems**: Virtual memory, bounded buffers, locks, and OS structure/Virtual Machines.
- **Networking**: Layering, routing, TCP, and datacenters/clouds.
- **Distributed Systems**: Reliability, logging, transactions, and replication (Raft).
- **Security**: Authentication, low-level attacks (Meltdown), and secure channels.
