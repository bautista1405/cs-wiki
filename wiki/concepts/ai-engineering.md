# AI Engineering

A specialized field of software engineering focused on integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) and other AI capabilities into software products.

## Core Competencies
- **LLM Orchestration**: Using frameworks (e.g., LangChain, LlamaIndex) to build complex AI workflows.
- **Data Retrieval**: Implementing RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) using vector databases.
- **Eval Frameworks**: Developing systematic ways to measure the quality and reliability of AI outputs.
