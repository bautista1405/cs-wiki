Main thesis: Mastery of patterns is the key to solving any LeetCode problem. 
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies key patterns like BFS, DFS, Two Pointers, Sliding Window.
- **The Roadmap**: Start with basics, then move to specific patterns, then Hard problems.
- **Consistency**: Solve 1-2 problems daily rather than cramming.
- **Goal**: Get to a point where you can look at a problem and instantly know which data structure to use.
