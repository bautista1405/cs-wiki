# Gergely Orosz - Shipping Projects

Insights on the importance of successfully shipping and completing projects.

## Key Insights
- The ability to "close the loop" and ship is as valuable as the ability to design.
- Execution excellence is a primary differentiator for high-performing engineers.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Shipping "fast" can lead to technical debt if not balanced with quality.
- **Data gaps**: Does not deeply explore the trade-offs between shipping speed and long-term maintainability.
