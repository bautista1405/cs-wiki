# Reflections on Performance

A reflective piece on the iterative process of optimizing software.

## Key Technical Points
- The "Measure, Analyze, Optimize" loop.
- Avoiding "premature optimization" by identifying the actual bottleneck through profiling.
- The diminishing returns of micro-optimizations.

## Integration
See [[performance-engineering]].

## Bias Checks
- **Counter-arguments**: In some real-time systems, optimization must be designed-in from the start, making the "measure later" approach risky.
- **Data gaps**: Does not provide specific tools for measurement, rather focuses on the mindset.
