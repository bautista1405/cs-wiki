# Source: Algorithms for Modern Hardware (Algorithmica)
Author: Sergey Slotin
Year: 2026
URL: https://en.algorithmica.org/hpc/
Summary: Technical resource focusing on high-performance computing (HPC) and the intersection of algorithm design with modern hardware characteristics.

## Key Contributions
- Shifts focus from asymptotic complexity (Big O) to real-world hardware performance.
- Detailed breakdown of ISA, SIMD parallelism, cache locality, and memory-level parallelism.
- Practical speedup benchmarks for standard algorithms (e.g., binary search, matrix multiplication).

## Bias and Gaps
- **Counter-arguments**: Focus on extreme optimization may lead to code that is less maintainable or portable across different hardware architectures.
- **Data gaps**: Currently based on a planned table of contents; full detailed implementation of all case studies may be pending.
