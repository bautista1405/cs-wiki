# Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces (OSTEP)

Authors: Remzi H. Arpaci-Dusseau and Andrea C. Arpaci-Dusseau (University of Wisconsin-Madison).

The book is centered around three conceptual pieces fundamental to operating systems:
1. Virtualization
2. Concurrency
3. Persistence

It covers CPU scheduling, memory management, and persistent storage.
