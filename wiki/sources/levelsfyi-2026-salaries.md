# Levels.fyi - Salaries and Levels

Data-driven mapping of engineering levels and compensation.

## Key Insights
- Standardizing "levels" across different companies helps in benchmarking.
- Compensation consists of base, stock, and bonus; the distribution varies by tier.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: Self-reported data can be skewed by "survivorship bias" or outliers.
- **Data gaps**: Lacks context on the specific performance expectations for each level.
