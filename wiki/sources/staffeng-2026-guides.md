# Staff Engineering Guides

Guidance and case studies for engineers transitioning into staff-level roles.

## Key Insights
- Staff roles are defined by impact rather than just technical skill.
- Focus on strategic alignment and organizational influence.

## Bias Check
- **Counter-arguments**: The definition of "Staff" varies wildly between companies (e.g., Google vs. small startups).
- **Data gaps**: Less emphasis on the transition from staff to principal/distinguished roles.
