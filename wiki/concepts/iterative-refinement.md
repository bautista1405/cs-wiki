    13|Iterative refinement is a strategy to ensure a working solution is on the table before attempting a complex optimization.
    14|
    15|### The Workflow
    16|1. **Brute Force**: State the most obvious, inefficient solution. This guarantees progress and provides a baseline.
    17|2. **Analysis**: Identify the bottleneck in the brute force approach (e.g., nested loops).
    18|3. **Optimization**: Apply a pattern (e.g., sorting, hashing) to reduce complexity.
    19|
    20|### Strategic Value
    21|It prevents the 'blank page' syndrome and allows the interviewer to see the candidate's logical progression from $O(n^2)$ to $O(n \log n)$ or $O(n)$.
